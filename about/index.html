ABOUT US

Revolution Records

The label was found by Ycmb Chuma and Kion. Founded on a commitment to boundary-breaking artistry and exceptional sound design, Revolution Records has been an active anchor for forward-thinking talent. We focus heavily on cultivation, offering independent creators an ecosystem to refine their craft and reach global listeners without compromised visions.

Our team works collectively across music publishing, record distribution, and visual media production to maintain high-quality aesthetic output. We believe inside every raw piece of art lies a story waiting to shift culture completely.
